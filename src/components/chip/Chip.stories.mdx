### Selectable
A selectable `Chip` acts like a (single) checkbox. A clickable icon to clear the selection is also shown.
<Canvas>
  <Story name="Selectable Chip">
    <Chip label="Selection" name="off" selectable />
  </Story>
  <Story name="Selectable Chip - checked">
    <Chip checked label="Selection" name="On" selectable />
  </Story>
</Canvas>
