## Types

`password`, telephone number (`tel`), `text`, `time`, and `url` are currently supported.

## Options
An `Input` can have help text, a helpfult icon, or an attached button. All of the options are
avaliable on each of the different types of `Input`. (And you may enable all of them at the same
time.) For the icon on the left of the `Input`, and in the button on the right, any of the
`SystemIcons` are available to you.
